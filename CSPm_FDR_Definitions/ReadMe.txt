In order to run any of these CSPm/FDR scripts you will require a copy
of the FDR checking system.

This is available from

https://cocotec.io/fdr/

The product is Licenced from the University of Oxford UK.

Depending on your affiliation you may or may not have to
pay a licence fee.

In order to observe the operation of any of the specifications you should type 
:probe name
at the FDR prompt.
Typically name should be the name of the system you wish to 
investigate, for example, System in most cases.  This will let you step through
the process network making choices as to which event is undertaken.  At each stage
the available events are indicated.
